Our Story






Trivia: When we are not chasing birds, we are channeling our energies into tech and higher ed spaces as engineers and writers. You can find more about us here and here, and reach out to us at birdphotographie@gmail.com.


Birders from San Francisco Bay Area, CA

We live in the city of Sunnyvale, a growing hub for technology and innovation at the heart of Santa Clara Valley, and also a town of family neighborhoods, parks, and diverse community of people.

We were born and raised in Kolkata — the City of Joy and the cultural capital of India — known for not only its literary, artistic, and revolutionary heritage, but also its architectural landmarks and culinary delights, including a myriad of street food that will leave most wanting for evermore.

We are explorers at heart — we love the outdoors and traveling. Our interest in active birdwatching started in 2020, around the time when COVID-19 hit, when we spent some soulful moments in nature. We loved that intimacy.

Among all the characters we interacted with during our nature immersion trips, it was birds that made their way into our lives. We decided to keep them forever, in stills and reels ðŸ“¸ðŸŽ¥

Sure, they are a delightful bunch of beauties, but as birders may well be aware, they are adept at constantly challenging the frontiers of human perseverance.

For hours on end, often with the sun blazing down, we survived all of their nonchalance, frantic fights, squabbles over food, foreplays, and those fine acrobatics. Our only arm of defense, a neat piece of lens.

Together we spotted many different species of birds — from starlings and western bluebirds, to iridescent hummingbirds, colorful warblers, all-white great egrets, and many more. We took photographs whenever possible and read about them later on.

Our interest grew over time as we got to learn more about the magnificent flying machines of nature. And we never stopped thereafter.
